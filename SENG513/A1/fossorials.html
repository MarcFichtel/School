Sterling
Ceratogyrus marshalli
Great Horned Baboon

  • Unicorn Tarantula (if it turns out to be a female).
  • Has a pretty bad reputation as being very defensive.
  • Gave a threat posture during his last rehousing.
  • Makes exquisite excavations.
  • Mean hunter.
  • Think there might be a horn forming on the head after the last molt. Keeping my fingers crossed!

Barry
Pterinochilus murinus
OBT (Orange Baboon Tarantula / Orange Bitey Thing)

  • It's the infamous OBT!
  • Pretty reclusive, as he is supposed to be.
  • Webs and digs a lot. Hides most of the time. About 1/2 inch large at this point in time.
  • Ecstatically takes down live prey larger than himself...
  • Has yet to show any of that beautiful orange.
  • Wanted to have one of these since the day I have been keeping T's.
